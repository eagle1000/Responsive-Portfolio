About Me

Hello, my name is Brett Artmann. I am a fullstack web developer working in the financial technology sector. Prior to working in developement, I spent 18 years as a commodity futures and options trader at the Chicago Board of Trade specializing in agricultural spread trading.

I am also the founder and CEO of the educational website Arbol ABC. Our website consists of over 300 learning games in Spanish for children ages 3-8. When not developing or creating educational games, I love spending time with my two Children, Christian (8) and Tessie (4). We love camping, biking, bowling and just having fun in the backyard!
